messageSendFailed

public void messageSendFailed(OutgoingMessageEvent event)
The message identified by the event could not be sent.

The cause of the failure, if any, is available from OutgoingMessageEvent.getFailure(). Failures while sending messages have several causes :

  • An IOException means that the messenger cannot send the message and the messenger will accept no further messages to be sent.

  • A RuntimeException mean that the message was not sent, but retries may or may not be possible based upon the status returned by Messenger.isClosed().

  • null means that the message was not sent, but may be retried. Usually the failure is due to local resource limits being exceeded. Attempts may be made to later resend the message, usually after waiting for congestion to clear.
